About me

Welcome to My travel blog! I'm Veronika, a passionate globetrotter on a mission to explore the world, immerse myself in diverse cultures, and capture unforgettable moments one handstand at a time. Travel has always been my greatest teacher, opening my eyes to the beauty and diversity of our planet. From bustling city streets to remote villages, I seek out authentic experiences that enrich my understanding of the world and its people.

One of my travel traditions? Taking a handstand photo in every destination I visit! It's my unique way of celebrating the joy and freedom of exploration while adding a touch of fun to my journeys.

Join me as I share travel tips, destination guides, cultural insights, and of course, my signature handstand photos from around the globe. Let's embark on this adventure together and inspire each other to wander fearlessly and embrace the beauty of our world. Remember, life is a journey meant to be explored with curiosity and enthusiasm!
